Wireframe Mock-Up

Computer Science 330

A wireframe prototype to redesign a fictional online book club app.

This app allows users to maintain "bookshelves" of books that they want to read, are reading now, and that they've finished reading.

They are able to add and remove books from the shelves, or move them from one shelf to another.

Feedback was positive — the Teacher's Assistant remarked, "What's impressive is that you kept things minimal and clean, despite adding many nice features."
